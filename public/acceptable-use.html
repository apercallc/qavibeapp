Acceptable Use Policy

Guidelines for appropriate and responsible use of QAVibe services.

Last updated: August 10, 2025

Our Commitment to Safe and Responsible Use

This Acceptable Use Policy outlines the guidelines for appropriate use of QAVibe services. By using our services, you agree to comply with this policy and help us maintain a safe, secure, and productive environment for all users.

1. General Principles

When using QAVibe services, you must:

  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
  • Respect the rights and safety of other users
  • Use services only for their intended purposes
  • Act with integrity and professionalism
  • Protect the security and availability of our systems

2. Prohibited Activities

Illegal or Harmful Activities

You may not use our services to:

  • Engage in any illegal activities or violate applicable laws
  • Harass, threaten, or harm other users
  • Distribute malware, viruses, or other malicious software
  • Conduct phishing, fraud, or deceptive practices
  • Violate intellectual property rights
  • Engage in money laundering or terrorist financing

Security and System Integrity

You are prohibited from:

  • Attempting to gain unauthorized access to our systems or data
  • Conducting security scans, penetration tests, or vulnerability assessments without permission
  • Interfering with or disrupting our services or infrastructure
  • Circumventing security measures or access controls
  • Reverse engineering, decompiling, or disassembling our software
  • Using automated tools to access our services without authorization

Data and Privacy Violations

  • Accessing, collecting, or processing personal data without consent
  • Sharing confidential or sensitive information inappropriately
  • Violating privacy rights of other users
  • Creating fake accounts or impersonating others
  • Scraping or harvesting user data without authorization

3. Content Guidelines

Prohibited Content

Do not upload, share, or transmit content that:

  • Is hateful, discriminatory, or promotes violence
  • Contains explicit sexual or graphic violent material
  • Infringes on intellectual property rights
  • Is defamatory, libelous, or misleading
  • Contains personal information of others without consent
  • Promotes illegal activities or substances

Test Data Responsibilities

When uploading test data or results:

  • Ensure you have the right to share the data
  • Remove or mask sensitive personal information
  • Comply with data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
  • Don't include proprietary or confidential business information
  • Verify that test data doesn't contain security vulnerabilities

4. Resource Usage

Fair Use

To ensure our services remain available to all users:

  • Use resources reasonably and avoid excessive consumption
  • Don't attempt to overload our systems or infrastructure
  • Implement efficient data transfer and storage practices
  • Report performance issues rather than attempting workarounds
  • Respect rate limits and usage quotas

API Usage

When using our APIs:

  • Follow documented API guidelines and best practices
  • Implement proper error handling and retry logic
  • Respect rate limits and avoid excessive requests
  • Use authentication tokens securely
  • Don't share API keys or access credentials

5. Commercial Use Guidelines

QAVibe services are free for all users, including commercial use, with these guidelines:

  • Commercial use is permitted and encouraged
  • Don't resell our services as your own product
  • Clearly identify QAVibe as the provider when showcasing results
  • Don't claim ownership of our intellectual property
  • Provide appropriate attribution when required

6. Reporting Violations

How to Report

If you encounter violations of this policy, please report them:

  • Abuse Reports: abuse@qavibeapp.com
  • Security Issues: security@qavibeapp.com
  • Legal Matters: legal@qavibeapp.com

Include in Your Report:

  • Detailed description of the violation
  • Evidence (screenshots, logs, URLs)
  • Date and time of the incident
  • Any relevant user account information

7. Enforcement

Investigation Process

When we receive reports of policy violations:

  • We will investigate promptly and thoroughly
  • We may temporarily suspend access during investigations
  • We will communicate with affected users when appropriate
  • We maintain confidentiality throughout the process

Potential Consequences

Violations may result in:

  • Warning: Written notice of policy violation
  • Content Removal: Deletion of violating content or data
  • Account Suspension: Temporary restriction of access
  • Account Termination: Permanent ban from our services
  • Legal Action: Referral to law enforcement when appropriate

8. Appeals Process

If you believe enforcement action was taken in error:

  • Contact us at appeals@qavibeapp.com
  • Provide detailed explanation and supporting evidence
  • Appeals will be reviewed by a different team member
  • We will respond within 5 business days

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Acceptable Use Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email and posted on our website. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Questions and Support

If you have questions about this policy or need guidance:

  • Support Team: support@qavibeapp.com
  • Legal Team: legal@qavibeapp.com
  • Legal Center: Visit Legal Center

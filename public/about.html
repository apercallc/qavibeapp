About QAVibe

We believe that high-quality testing tools should be accessible to everyone, regardless of budget constraints.

Our Mission

QAVibe was founded with a simple but powerful mission: to democratize access to enterprise-grade testing tools. We've seen too many talented developers and small teams struggle with limited resources while trying to maintain high software quality standards.

Why We're Different

Truly Free

No hidden costs, no freemium limitations, no "pay to unlock" features. Everything is completely free.

Built by Developers

Created by developers who understand the real challenges of software testing and quality assurance.

Community Driven

We listen to our users and continuously improve based on real-world feedback and needs.

Our Products

We currently offer two powerful platforms:

TestFlux

Our test results dashboard aggregates, stores, and visualizes automated test results from popular testing frameworks like Playwright, ZAP, and k6. Perfect for teams who want enterprise-level insights without enterprise-level costs.

StackHealth

Our comprehensive software quality assessment platform provides scorecards, analytics, PDF reports, and product management capabilities. Get a complete picture of your software stack's health and security posture.
